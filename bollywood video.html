PUSHPA 2



OVERVIEW


*Pushpa 2: The Rule*, directed by Sukumar, is the sequel to the blockbuster *Pushpa: The Rise*. Starring Allu Arjun as Pushpa Raj, it continues the story of Pushpa's rise as a powerful figure in the underworld of red sandalwood smuggling. Facing intensified conflicts with police officer Bhanwar Singh Shekhawat (Fahadh Faasil) and new adversaries, the film delivers action-packed sequences and emotional moments. With Rashmika Mandanna reprising her role as Srivalli, the movie emphasizes Pushpa's growing empire and personal struggles.
